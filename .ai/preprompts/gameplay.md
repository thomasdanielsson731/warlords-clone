# Gameplay Rules

Always preserve the original Warlords II gameplay loop:

1. Start with one city and one hero
2. Capture nearby neutral cities
3. Produce units
4. Build front lines
5. Explore ruins and temples
6. Gain artifacts and stronger armies
7. Conquer most of the map

Important design principles:
- Neutral cities should dominate the early game
- Cities are more important than individual units
- Heroes are rare and powerful
- Combat should be simple but meaningful
- Expansion should feel exciting
- Factions must feel distinct
- The player should always want "one more turn"

Never introduce:
- Real-time gameplay
- Deep RPG systems
- Crafting
- Complex economy simulation
- Hundreds of stats

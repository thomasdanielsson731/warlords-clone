# Visual Style

The game should be a modern premium remake of Warlords II.

Target references:
- Songs of Conquest
- Age of Wonders 4
- Northgard

Visual goals:
- Slightly angled top-down or isometric camera
- Dark fantasy atmosphere
- Large detailed cities
- Smaller readable armies
- Painterly terrain
- Warm lighting
- Fog and atmosphere
- Smooth camera movement
- Smooth unit movement
- Distinct faction identity

Faction style:
- Humans: stone castles, blue banners
- Orcs: wood, spikes, fire
- Elves: elegant white towers and forests
- Lord Bane: dark stone and purple light

Avoid:
- Programmer placeholder visuals
- Flat colors
- Tiny unreadable units
- Generic fantasy style

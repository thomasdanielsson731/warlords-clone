# Graphics Roadmap

## Phase 1: Primitive 3D Shapes ✅
- 20x20 terrain grid with colored boxes (grass/forest/mountain/water)
- Units as colored cylinders with faction colors
- Cities as small towers with battlements and faction flags
- Ruins as pillars with mystery glow
- Angled top-down camera with orbit controls
- Fog and basic lighting
- Selection ring on selected unit
- Movement range highlight on terrain
- Hero crown indicator (gold octahedron)

## Phase 2: Better Visuals
- Terrain textures (grass, dirt, stone, water shader)
- Improved city models per faction (stone castle, orc fort, elf tower, dark citadel)
- Animated units (idle bobbing, movement lerp)
- Tree models for forests (multiple variations)
- Water animation (subtle wave shader)
- Better lighting with shadows

## Phase 3: Faction Architecture & Effects
- Real faction-specific architecture for cities
- Particle effects (combat sparks, ruin exploration magic)
- Dynamic lighting (day/night, faction auras)
- Animated flags and banners
- Unit death/spawn animations
- Combat camera zoom
- Minimap overlay

# Warlords II Canon Rules

- Each faction starts with:
  - 1 city
  - 1 level 1 hero

- Most cities on the map are neutral.

- Cities continuously produce the selected unit until changed.

- Cities may offer up to four unit types.

- Captured cities may be:
  - occupied
  - pillaged
  - sacked
  - razed

- Cities provide a defense bonus.

- Heroes:
  - gain experience
  - can carry items
  - can search ruins
  - can receive quests
  - are capped at level 5

- Only heroes may search ruins.

- Ruins may grant:
  - gold
  - artifact
  - ally
  - map reveal
  - nothing

- Diplomacy matters:
  - attacking neutral or allied players causes diplomatic penalties
  - early wars can provoke all factions

- Fog of war is symmetrical:
  - AI only knows what it has explored

- Victory is normally:
  - control the majority of cities

## Advanced Rules

- Heroes cannot be produced in cities.
- New heroes can only be hired.
- Heroes may only have one active quest.
- Veteran armies can gain up to +4 strength.
- Cities can forward production to another city.
- City capture should allow occupy, sack, pillage or raze.
- Attacking while at peace should trigger diplomatic consequences.
